Discord Documentation Repository

Captain Bot

A single, reliable Discord bot to manage, auto-moderate and automate your server!

Logging

Captain's logging is highly customisable and designed to be easy to read without taking up a large amount of room on your screen!

Lockdown

When the chat gets too heated, let it chill out with a lockdown! With Captain, Admins can easily lock a public channel for a certain length of time or until someone cancels it!

Starboards

Starboards allow users to upvote certain posts and eventually they'll be sent to a special channel for safe keeping. Everything is highly customisable so you can even use a custom emoji if you want!

Starred messages sent in an NSFW channel are treated differently from normal messages. You'll only see attachments as links rather than a big image, all text will be placed within a spoiler and "NSFW" will be written at the top of the message!

Reaction Roles

Need a way for users to pick between some vanity roles? Perhaps some coloured roles? Not to worry, Captain makes it easy to do this! You can use unicode emojis, custom emojis or a mix of both if you want!

Message Archives

Need to keep proof that a server member broke x, y and z rules? Well, Captain can help with that! When creating an archive, Captain takes a bunch of messages, does some internal work, and gives you a HTML file.

HTML files are basically a webpage. So, all you have to do is download a file and you have a neat little UI that you can use as evidence against someone! It even has a button to save everything as a raw .txt file!

Infractions

Nothing is more crucial to a Moderator's arsenal than basic infraction commands! It's for this reason that we took extra care to make sure Captain's infraction commands are perfect for any moderation situation!

Moderators can search for infractions by case ID, actor ID or target ID as well as display the most recent 10 infractions! Admins can also delete infractions.

Roles for Badges

Aren't you just sick and tired of giving roles to every single member that has a badge on their profile? Well it's time to automate it! Captain's "Roles for Badges" system will automatically assign roles to new users according to their profile flags.
